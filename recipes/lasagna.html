LASAGNA

INGREDIENTS

Bolognese Sauce

  • Tomato Paste
  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 cup Whole Milk
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Celery Stalk
  • 1/4 lb. Bacon or Pancetta
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 4 cups Chicken Stock, homemade or store bought
  • Parmesan

Beschamel

  • 2 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Flour
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Nutmeg
  • Parmesan

General

  • 1 lb. Lasagna Sheets (Do NOT get oven-ready option)
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Pepper

STEPS

Making the Bolognese

  1. Heat up dutch oven
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. While the meat is browning, make your sofrito by blending carrot, onion, celery and garlic
  4. Once meat is browned, take it out and put it in a bowl to the side
  5. Discard oil until you have about 1 tsp left
  6. Cook bacon/pancetta and render fat
  7. Add in blended carrot, onion, celery, and garlic mix
  8. Cook this until it starts to stick at the bottom of the pan
  9. Once you start to see it sticking, deglaze pot with white wine
  10. Cook off alcohol and let it simmer
  11. Once it's cooked off, add in tomato paste and cook until it starts to stick at the bottom and is bright red
  12. After cooking tomato paste, put in chicken stock and milk
  13. Simmer this until consistency of sauce is like a sloppy joe

Making the Beschamel

  1. Warm up butter
  2. Cook flour in butter
  3. Once the flour is browned and cooked, slowly pour in milk
  4. Whisk as you put milk into roux
  5. Season with salt, pepper, parmesan and add in nutmeg

Building the Lasagna

  1. Cook off pasta, maybe 1-2 minutes before al dente
  2. Drain pasta and set to the side with your beschamel and bolognese
  3. For assembly, you will put down a layer of bolognese as the first layer
  4. Then put in pasta sheets to cover the entire pan
  5. Layer bolognese sauce to cover the lasagna sheets and add dollops of beschamel on top
  6. Continue to follow this layering pattern until you get to the top
  7. Cover the last layer with parmesan
  8. Bake at 450 for 45 minutes
  9. Serve qween
